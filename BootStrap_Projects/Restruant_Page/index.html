About Lingayath Idily Hotel

Lingayath Idily Hotel is the best South Indian restaurant brand with more than a decade of experience serving authentic and contemporary
delicacies of South Indian cuisine. Lingayath Idily Hotel has chosen the franchising system of business expansion to spread its mouth-watering food across the globe.


Lingayath Idily Hotel is the fastest-growing franchisee in India and overseas. Lingayath Idily Hotel is inviting like-minded entrepreneurs who wish to join the ever-growing family to serve hungry customers across the globe.Mr. Idli offers restaurant business opportunities anywhere in India and Overseas. Start your restaurant business with our highly profitable and attractive franchising models with good returns. You can choose to diversify from your present boring business or employment and start an exciting journey with Mr. Idli’s restaurant business.

Anyone who desires to serve healthy and delicious food and beverage delicacies can start Mr. Idli’s restaurant in their neighborhood.
Even retired government officials, home-makers, and freshers can successfully manage the Mr. Idli restaurant franchise business.
